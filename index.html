What Is A Robot?

A robot is a programmable machine designed to carry out specific tasks autonomously or semi-autonomously. It typically consists of mechanical components, sensors, actuators, and a control system, enabling it to interact with its environment.

Importance of Robotics

Robotics is a field that holds immense importance in today's rapidly advancing world. It combines science, engineering, and technology to create intelligent machines capable of performing complex tasks with precision and efficiency.

Applications of Robotics

Robotics combines science, engineering, and technology to create intelligent machines. From manufacturing to healthcare and space exploration, robots perform complex tasks with precision, revolutionizing industries and enhancing our lives.
